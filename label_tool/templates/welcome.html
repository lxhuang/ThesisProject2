Thank you for participating in our human behavior study. In this study, you are going to watch 14 videos (each is about 2~3 minutes long). Each video features a person's nonverbal reactions when s/he was watching a video. Your task is to label the occurrence of head nod in each video as accurately as possible.

In order to label the videos, you have to use the tool shown as below. Here is a short tutorial:
  1. Press the space bar to start loading the video. The loading progress will be shown in the Box 1.
  2. After the video is loaded, press the space bar to start playing the video.
  3. While the video is playing, pay attention to the occurrence of head nod. Head nod usually lasts for a period of time.
  4. At the beginning of each head nod, press the space bar and hold it; release the space bar until the head nod ends.
  5. After you finish watching the video, you can adjust the labels by dragging on their boundaries. When you replay the videos, you can change to playback rate to 0.5X, 1.5X or 2.0X.
